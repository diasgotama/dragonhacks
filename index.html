FAQ

  • Is Dragon Hacks Awesome?

    Yes, it is!

  • What is this 'hardware hackathon'?

    Our hardware hackathon will be 24 hours of working in teams to create a ‘hardware hack’. Basically you will be brainstorming ideas with your team on a project you can create centered around hardware we have available. You will get as much accomplished as you can, and at the end of the 24 hours there will be judging and prizes awarded to the best teams projects. A list of hardware can be found above.

  • When is it?

    January 10th-11th, with a start time at 1pm.

  • Where is it?

    It will take place in Drexel’s engineering building The Edmund D. Bossone Research Enterprise Center, located on the south side of Market Street between 31st and 32nd Streets.

  • Why should I come?

    a. Yes, spending 24 hours working on a project outside of school may seem a little daunting- but we promise you will not regret it! First you will make tons of fun memories, meet new people, make a lot of new connections, get free stuff, free food, meet corporate sponsors, and potentially win big prizes. Secondly, on a more serious note, you will further develop your skills in coding, working with hardware, and working on a project as a team. Lots of employers are looking for projects students do outside of school- its interesting, shows passion, unique, and something to talk about during interviews!

  • What should I bring to the hackathon?

    Valid student ID, laptop, charger, whatever makes you comfortable or supports your coding activities. (keyboard, mouse, etc.). There will be an area to take naps, so feel free to also bring pillows/blankets/sleeping bags etc.

  • What I should not bring to the hackathon?

    Firearms, knives, and weapons of any kind are banned. If you can't bring it on an airplane, don't bring it here!

  • Who can attend?

    The hackathon is open for all current university students (grad / undergrad). You do not need prior hackathon or experience with hardware to attend.

  • How is the application process?

    Just register on the site! If space becomes limited, we will review applications. You will receive confirmation of registration via email. Registration is individual, and teams can be formed the day of the event.

  • How big is the team ?

    Minimum size is 2, max is 6. From experience, optimal time size is around 3 / 4 people. You can make a team before the event or make it during the hackathon.

  • Do you provide hardware ?

    Yes, a lot. Items can be found above.

  • How much will this cost me?

    Nothing! Dinner, lunch, breakfast , drinks and snacks are on us! As well as T-shirts and other swag. If you have questions regarding travel reimbursement, please contact us.
